# Focus Hub 🎯

> *One place. One goal. Everything that matters — right now.*

---

## The Hook

Have you ever felt scattered across a dozen different apps, trying to juggle learning, work, personal stuff, and networking — all at the same time?

Coming into the professional industry as a junior analyst, I felt completely overwhelmed. I was working a nine-to-five, then coming home and trying to build my portfolio, study on Coursera, post on LinkedIn, take care of my kids, manage my house — all at once, across all different platforms.

No structure. No direction. Just noise.

I was busy every single day — but I wasn't productive. There's a big difference between being busy and actually moving forward.

So I built Focus Hub.

**You wake up. Instead of checking five different apps, instead of scrolling for hours being unproductive — you open Focus Hub. It shows you exactly what to do first, based on your actual goals, not just what's screaming loudest in your head. You do that. You move forward. You see progress. No more spinning wheels. No more procrastination. Only forward momentum.**

It's a free, open system that anyone juggling multiple things in life can use and improve together — interlocking your whole life, not just your work.

## How It Works

Start with your goal — your mind map.

Break it down into categories. Break those into tasks. Every single thing you do, whether it's personal, professional, or learning, connects back to that same goal.

**Nothing is wasted. Everything moves you forward.**

```
Your Goal (Mind Map)
    └── Category (e.g. Analytics Learning)
          └── Subcategory (e.g. Tableau)
                └── Task (e.g. Complete Dashboard Project)
    └── Category (e.g. Networking)
          └── Task (e.g. Post on LinkedIn today)
    └── Category (e.g. Personal)
          └── Task (e.g. School run, groceries)
```

Everything funnels toward the same goal. You always know where you stand.

## What's Coming Next

### Version 1 — The Foundation ✅
The learning phase. Built the basic tracker, tested automations, proved the concept works. Timestamps, task IDs, KPI calculations — all functioning. Lessons learned about architecture and planning before building.

### Version 2 — Phase 1 (Current) 🔨
What we're building now. A unified dashboard with the mind map connected to your goals and a priority system that tells you exactly what to do today.

### Version 3 — Scale 🚀
The scaled version. Pulls from GitHub, Google Calendar, and other platforms. Open for collaboration and community improvements. Built together.
